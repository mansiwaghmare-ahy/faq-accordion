FAQ's

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor is an online platform that provides real-world coding challenges for web developers. It helps users improve their HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills by building projects from design mockups. Developers can also receive feedback and compare solutions with others in the community.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor gives you many challenges for free, but some projects and features are behind a paid Pro subscription. The majority of their coding challenges are available without paying, and you can practice and build projects with those free ones. Premium challenges and certain extras (like advanced design files) require a Pro membership.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can use Frontend Mentor projects in your portfolio. In fact, that's one of the main purposes of the platform to let developers showcase real-world projects they've built from the designs.
